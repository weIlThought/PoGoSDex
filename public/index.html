PGSharp FAQ

  • What is PGSharp?

    PGSharp is a tool for spoofing location in Pokémon GO on Android devices.

  • Is PGSharp safe to use?

    Use at your own risk. Spoofing may violate Niantic's terms of service.

  • How do I install PGSharp?

    Visit the official PGSharp website and follow the installation instructions for your device.

Features

  • Joystick for movement

    Move your avatar freely on the map using a virtual joystick.

  • Teleportation

    Jump instantly to any location worldwide.

  • Auto-walk

    Automatically walk a route for hatching eggs or collecting items.

Known Issues

  • App crashes on some devices

    Try updating PGSharp or your device firmware.

  • Location not updating

    Check location permissions and restart the app.
